Chili Popcorn

Description

Get ready to spice up your snack game with a fiery twist on a classic favorite—chili popcorn! This recipe takes the humble popcorn kernel and transforms it into a bold, zesty treat that's perfect for movie nights, game days, or anytime you’re craving a crunchy, savory bite with a kick. Infused with the rich warmth of chili powder, a hint of smoky paprika, and a dash of cayenne for extra heat, every kernel is a flavor explosion. The best part? It’s quick, easy to make, and endlessly customizable to suit your spice tolerance. So grab your ingredients, fire up the stovetop, and let’s pop to it!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • ½ cup white popcorn kernels
  • ½ teaspoon popcorn salt, divided
  • ⅛ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon onion powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground chipotle chile pepper
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground ancho chile pepper

Directions

Step 1:

Heat canola oil in a large pot over medium-high heat; add 1 popcorn kernel to hot oil. The oil is ready when kernel pops.

Step 2:

Pour popcorn kernels into hot oil; season with 1/2 of the popcorn salt. Stir to coat. Cover the pot with a sheet of aluminum foil. Poke about 10 holes into the aluminum foil using a knife. Cook popcorn until all kernels are popped, 5 to 10 minutes. Remove the pot from heat.

Step 3:

Sprinkle popcorn with remaining popcorn salt, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, chipotle chile pepper, and ancho chile pepper. Toss to evenly coat.
